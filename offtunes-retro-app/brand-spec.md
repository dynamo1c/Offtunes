# OFFTUNES Brand Spec

**Vibe:** Vintage handheld device / 1989 Japanese Casio calculator / Pastel pink stationery. Mechanical, airy, structured.

## Colors (OKLch/Hex)
- `--bg`: #FAF5F7 (App background, warm white with pink tint)
- `--surface`: #F2E8ED (Cards, panels, drawers)
- `--surface-raised`: #EDD9E5 (Button faces, knob surfaces, raised elements)
- `--pink`: #F4B8CC (Pastel pink, borders, inactive states, soft fills)
- `--rose`: #C2577A (Primary accent, active states, highlights, CTAs)
- `--mauve`: #8B3A55 (Deep accent, pressed states, strong labels, icons)
- `--text`: #3A1F2B (Primary text, dark plum)
- `--text-soft`: #9B7080 (Secondary text, metadata, placeholders)
- `--lcd`: #B8E0C8 (Soft mint green on cream panel, vintage Casio screen style)
- `--error`: #D94F6A (Failed downloads, errors)

## Typography
- **Display:** Share Tech Mono (Song titles, timestamps, readouts)
- **UI:** DM Sans (Tight, geometric, warm)

## Posture
- 4px grid. 4px border-radius (slightly softened hardware).
- 1.5px solid `--pink` for panels. 2px solid `--rose` for active states.
- Buttons look like physical keycaps (raised bottom edge that collapses on press).
- Flat hardware sliders with rectangular thumbs.
- No drop shadows, no glassmorphism, everything feels silk-screened.
